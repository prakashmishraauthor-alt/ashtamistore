Jewellery Care Instructions

  • Avoid direct contact with perfumes, hairsprays, or body lotions.
  • Put on your jewellery last after completing makeup and hair prep.
  • Wipe gently with a soft cotton cloth after every single use.
  • Store in airtight zip-lock pouches or our original padded box.
  • Keep separated from other metallic jewellery pieces to avoid scratches.
  • Do not clean with harsh chemical solvents or liquid soap mixes.
  • Remove before swimming, exercising, or heading into hot showers.
  • Keep away from heavy direct heat and continuous humid conditions.
