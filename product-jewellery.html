Jewellery Care Tips

  • Store jewellery in a clean and dry place.
  • Keep each piece separately to avoid scratches.
  • Avoid direct contact with perfumes and chemicals.
  • Remove jewellery before bathing or swimming.
  • Clean gently with a soft, dry cloth after use.
  • Keep away from excessive moisture for long-lasting shine.
