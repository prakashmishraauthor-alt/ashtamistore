The Warmth of Tradition in Every Bite

Sonth Ladoo is one of India's most cherished traditional winter delicacies, prepared using dried ginger (sonth), pure ghee, edible gum (gond), whole wheat flour and a carefully selected blend of premium dry fruits. Every batch is handcrafted in small quantities to preserve freshness, authentic taste and nutritional value.

Prepared using traditional family recipes, Sonth Ladoo offers a perfect balance of flavour and wellness. The natural warmth of dried ginger combined with the richness of ghee and nuts makes it an excellent seasonal sweet enjoyed by families across generations.

At Ashtami, every Sonth Ladoo is prepared with premium ingredients, hygienic practices and the care that only homemade food can provide.
